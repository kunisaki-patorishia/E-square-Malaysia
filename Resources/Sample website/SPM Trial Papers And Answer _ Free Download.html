Have question?

How can I join the SPM Free Past Year Papers group?

You can join our SPM Whatsapp group by registering through the SPM paper@E-Square platform. Once registered, you’ll receive a link to access all the free resources, including past year papers, answer schemes, and videos.

Are all the past year papers and answer schemes free?

Yes, all SPM past year papers and answer schemes available in the group are completely free. You can download them and use them to enhance your study preparations.

Are there explanation videos for all subjects?

Currently, we provide explanation videos for key subjects like Mathematics, Science, and Bahasa Malaysia. We are continuously working on adding more videos for other subjects.

Can I ask questions about specific exam questions in the group?

Yes! Our Telegram group allows students to ask questions. You can post any doubts you have about the exam papers, and our tutors or other students will help clarify them.

Do I need to pay to access the video explanations?

No, all video explanations are free of charge. We provide these resources to help students better understand complex exam questions and improve their overall performance.

Is the group only for SPM students, or can others join as well?

The group is primarily focused on SPM students, but anyone looking for educational resources, like teachers or parents supporting their children, are welcome to join. Our goal is to provide helpful study tools for everyone involved in the SPM preparation process.

Can I contribute my own past year questions or videos?

Yes, we welcome contributions from users. If you have past year questions or educational videos that you would like to share, please contact us through our website or group for further instructions on how to contribute.
